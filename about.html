Face of campus Nigeria is a beauty,intelligence and artistic contest envision the positive attributes of Nigerian youths across the country. Face of campus Nigeria is an independent and unbiased event that aims to showcase the cultural diversity of Nigeria; hence the participation is open for all, male and female.
